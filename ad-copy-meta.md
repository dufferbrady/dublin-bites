### Ad 1 – Founding Members only (Scarcity)

**Primary texts**

1. **“Founding Members get what regular subscribers never will.”**  
   Dublin’s first food deals newsletter is opening to a limited group. Weekly restaurant deals, hidden gems and what’s on — free. For the first 100 people, Founding Member status is permanent: priority access, subscriber‑only offers, and your name on the list forever. After that, you’re just another subscriber.

2. **“The first 100 Dubliners get in. Everyone else comes after.”**  
   Dublin Bites sends one email a week with the best value spots, not just the noisiest ones on your feed. Founding Members see the deals first and get perks no one else does. Once those 100 spots are filled, the door closes.

3. **“This isn’t just a newsletter — it’s a tier you can never buy later.”**  
   Founding Members lock in permanent status by joining early. That means early access to deals, exclusive offers and recognition in every issue. Miss it now and you’ll never be a Founding Member, no matter how long you’re subscribed.

4. **“You can join any newsletter tomorrow. You can only be a Founding Member today.”**  
   Dublin Bites is starting with 100 people who get lifetime perks: priority deal access, subscriber‑only specials and a permanent badge. When the list hits 100, the sign‑up stays, but the status disappears.

5. **“Right now, you’re still early enough to matter.”**  
   Early subscribers decide what Dublin Bites becomes — and they get rewarded for it. If you want the deals, you can always join later. If you want the Founding Member treatment, you have to join before the 100th person does.

**Headline**

> Dublin’s first food deals newsletter — Founding Members only

**Description (optional)**

> Free weekly deals. First 100 lock in Founding Member status.

**CTA**

> Sign Up
