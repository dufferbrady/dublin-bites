Privacy Policy – Dublin Bites

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Dublin Bites is a newsletter and service that shares food deals, new openings, and related updates for people in Dublin. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at hello@dublinbites.ie.

What data we collect

When you join as a Founding Member or sign up to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Email address – so we can send you Dublin Bites emails.

When you visit our site, we and our service providers may also collect technical data such as:

  • Pages you visit and actions you take on the site.
  • Approximate location, device, and browser information.
  • Identifiers used by marketing and analytics tools (for example, cookies and similar technologies).

How we use your data

We use your information to:

  • Send you the Dublin Bites newsletter and related food deal updates.
  • Let you know about partner offers, events, and new openings we think you will like.
  • Understand how people use our site so we can improve what we offer.
  • Run advertising and retargeting campaigns (for example, on Meta or other platforms) so we can find more people who may be interested in Dublin Bites.

Legal basis (GDPR)

For people in the EU/EEA/UK, we rely on:

  • Consent – when you actively sign up with your email address to receive Dublin Bites emails.
  • Legitimate interests – to understand site usage and run limited marketing and analytics, where this does not override your rights and freedoms. Where required, we will ask for your consent before setting certain cookies or similar technologies.

Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Service providers who help us run Dublin Bites, such as hosting, database, and infrastructure providers (including Netlify and Netlify DB/Neon).
  • Analytics and advertising partners where we place pixels or similar technologies (for example, Meta). These partners may act as independent controllers of some technical and usage data.

How long we keep your data

We keep your email address for as long as you are subscribed to Dublin Bites and for a reasonable period afterwards, for example to keep a record of who opted out. We keep technical and analytics data for as long as needed for the purposes described above, or as required by our service providers.

Your rights

If you are in the EU/EEA/UK, you have rights under data protection law, including the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Ask us to delete your data in certain circumstances.
  • Object to or ask us to restrict certain types of processing.
  • Ask for a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, or to ask questions about this policy, contact us at hello@dublinbites.ie. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, let you know through the site or by email.
